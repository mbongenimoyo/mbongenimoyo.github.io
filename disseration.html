TensorFlow Project

For my final year research, I decided to delve into an area in computer science that I had no previous experience in, which was “deep learning”. This had always been an area of intrigue for me because from an outsider’s perspective it always seemed somewhat magical. Even before the final year of University had started I had started reading and watching videos about neural networks and their implementations. After doing this for several weeks and I wanted to see if these technologies could be used to return a profit from predicting the outcome of Football games.At the end of my disseration the NN i had trained managed to return a profit of 5% after 150 bets of premeier games.

Technologies- Python, TensorFlow.
